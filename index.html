Ken & Jam

Our Love Story...

When we were young, we both did not imagine that God would bring us closer to the one who we’ll share our lives with. We both have our own shares of heartbreaks, walked different paths, taking separate turns, making distinct decisions in life so we could reach our individual goals and dream. But who would have thought that everything we have made with our lives would convince God that we really both belong to each other. When we met, it’s like God answered why we didn’t work out with somebody else. Everything just got it’s meaning, we got motivation to become better for each other, resolving individual issues to make the relationship work… and all those hardwork and every challenges we’ve gone through was all worth the wait. Because, finally, we decided to make it official before God and those we love as we celebrate our love on our special day.

I do!

Mark Kenneth Ortiz01.01.1992

When I met Kenneth, there’s nothing really special at first. Little did I know that I already met the man that will make me head over heels in love.

Before I met him, I was into rockstar, cool type of guys but when he came into my life, he made me realize that he was exactly who I am looking for. I was hoping to find love in the wrong places and chasing the wrong things. God has truly led me to where my heart really belongs to.

Now that we have each other, it’s time to explore life in completely different perspective with much love, joy and hope.

Me too!

Jamielyn Floralde12.11.1989

Others say, you need to wait patiently for your one true love. But I know this girl has a very lil patience in her body.

So instead of letting her to fall on the wrong guy while waiting for me, I just to ask her "Can you be with me until God does us apart" and without even a wink of an eye she said "Yes, I do" then tears fall from her eyes. And that's the start of our love journey.

Jam is really a very smart, tough and strong lady (yes, she is a lady). But deep inside of her, you will see a very precious, kind hearted and loving girl grown into a very beautiful woman. In fact, she is the sweetest girl I ever knew.

I am truly blessed that God answered my prayer by having her beside me forever. And now, I'm so excited to spend every day for the rest of my life with my wife!

With all humility and gentleness, with patience, bearing with one another in love.
- ephians 4:2
2012

19 April: How they met

It looked like a boxing match, ain’t it? but it’s not…

Every year, LOJ Youth organizes summer Camp CALYE. And way back 2012, it’s called KNOCKOUT

Where they both attended and were grouped into the same team (that’s how destiny played out) with the sole purpose of finding oneself and spiritual maturity.

During the adventure and lesson filled camp, not only did they acquired the will and fire to take on life, but also they found friends and families that became integral part of their spiritual lives.

However, little did they know that God’s plans for their lives together was also set in motion.


22 April: The First zipline together

This is their first picture together, without knowing this Zipline will let them fall in love to one another.

8 September: Starry Starry Night

Since their first meeting, not only their friendship grew stronger and deeper but also they found their strength in God individually. They both serve LOJ Youth Missions trying to help others to know more about Jesus.

But somewhere along the way, Kenneth had developed his feelings for Jam. And he believed he could only court someone if he is ready to marry and be with her for the rest of his life.

So after a very long time of prayer and discernment from God, he makes up his mind and decided to confess about his feelings.

It was a starry evening and at the rooftop, it started as simple sharing of stories much about anything they could think of but he also took the moment to lay out his dreams and plans in the future. Then he ask Jam “Pwede ba kitang makasama habang buhay? Gusto ko sanang abutin ang mga pangarap ko kasama ka...” (Can I be with you forever? I would like to reach my dreams with you). Jam was so silent while tears fall from her eyes.

That night was both unforgettable and a very touching moment in their lives.

12 December: Officially together

When you know it’s right, you just can’t wait to keep it yours forever. This day began their awesome years of being together standing up for each other no matter what they encounter in life. They felt just right and on point and nothing could stop them from showing their love for each other.

2015

12 December: She said yes!

After days, months and years passed, they had their share of many ups and downs, and until the time comes that he decided to put a ring on the woman he was madly and truly in love with.

It was a surreal moment when he knelt and popped the much awaited question of them all… and she said YES!

Everything was magical and everyone wished them well with the coming months of wedding preparation.
